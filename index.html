About Me

I am a full-stack web developer who has been working in industry since January 2015. I am currently building enterprise level web applications in PHP and Javascript, primarily using Symfony and Backbone/Marionette. While I do both and front- and back-end work, my real passion and focus is for working on the front-end developing rich, dynamic user interfaces.

I am always excited to learn new languages, frameworks and patterns. At the moment I am really loving Vue JS and their Flux-like architecture, Vuex. I am also enjoying re-acquainting myself with Python, after an almost 2 year break.

Outside of work I like to engage in various active pursuits - mainly cycling, but I have been known to dabble in climbing, running and swimming too. I also enjoy books and films - as I am writing this I am currently reading SPQR by Mary Beard and the last film I watched was Wiener-Dog.
